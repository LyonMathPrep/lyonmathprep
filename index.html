Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about Lyon Math Prep.

Is this really completely free?

Yes! Lyon Math Prep is a 100% non-profit organization. We don't charge anything, ever. All our tutors are volunteers who want to help students succeed.

Who are the tutors?

Our tutors are high school and college students who excel in math and are passionate about teaching. They're trained and supervised to ensure quality instruction in our small group classes.

How do I sign up?

Click "Student Portal" in the menu, create an account, and fill out a quick form about your needs. We'll match you with a tutor within 2-3 days.

What if I need to cancel a class?

No problem! Just let us know at least 24 hours in advance through the student portal messaging system. You can join another scheduled class that fits your availability.

How many students are in each class?

We keep our classes small with 3-5 students per session. This allows for personalized attention while also creating a collaborative learning environment.

How long are tutoring sessions?

Standard sessions are 45-60 minutes, but we can adjust based on your needs and schedule. Most students attend 1-2 classes per week.
