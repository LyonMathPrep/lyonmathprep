Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about Lyon Math Prep's free online tutoring.

Is this really completely free?

Yes! Lyon Math Prep is a 100% non-profit organization based in South Lyon, MI. We don't charge anything, ever. All our online math tutors are volunteers who want to help students succeed.

Who are the tutors?

Our tutors are high school and college students from South Lyon, Novi, Canton, and surrounding areas who excel in math and are passionate about teaching. They're trained and supervised to ensure quality instruction in our small group classes.

How do I sign up?

Click the red 'Student Portal' button above, and fill out a quick form about your needs. We'll match you with a tutor within 2-3 days and send you your login information.

How many students are in each class?

We keep our classes small with 3-5 students per session. This allows for personalized attention while also creating a collaborative learning environment.

How long are tutoring sessions?

Standard sessions are 45-60 minutes, but we can adjust based on your needs and schedule. Most students attend 1-2 classes per week.
