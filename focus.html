Privacy & Data Handling

  • 100% On-Device Processing: All pose and face detection runs locally in your browser via MediaPipe. No video frames are ever sent to a server.
  • Zero Data Storage: We do not save, cache, or log video frames. Image data exists only in temporary memory and is overwritten instantly frame-by-frame.
  • Explicit Consent: The camera only activates when you click "Start Session" and requests your browser's explicit permission.
  • User Control: You can pause or stop the session at any time. Stopping the session immediately kills the camera stream.
