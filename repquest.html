Camera & Privacy

RepQuest needs camera access to track your workout. Here's what happens:

  • On-device only: Video is processed in your browser by MediaPipe Pose. Frames never leave your device.
  • Nothing stored: Each frame is analyzed in memory and immediately discarded. No images or video are saved.
  • Only numbers saved: Rep counts and XP are stored in localStorage. Clear browser data to remove everything.
  • You're in control: Pause or stop anytime. Closing the tab ends all processing.

No backend. No accounts. No telemetry.
