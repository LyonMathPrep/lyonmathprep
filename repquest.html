Camera & Privacy

To track your workout, REPQUEST needs camera access. Here's exactly what happens:

  • On-device only: Video is processed in your browser by MediaPipe Pose. Frames never leave your device.
  • Nothing stored: Each frame is analyzed in memory and immediately discarded. No images, video, or recordings are saved anywhere.
  • Only numbers saved: Rep counts and XP are stored locally in your browser's localStorage. Clear browser data to remove everything.
  • You're in control: Pause or stop anytime. The camera indicator shows when analysis is active. Closing this tab ends all processing.

No backend. No accounts. No telemetry. Just your browser, your camera, your workout.
