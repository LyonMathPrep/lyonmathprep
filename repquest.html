Camera & Privacy

RepQuest needs camera access to count your reps.

  • On-device only. Video processed by MediaPipe Pose in your browser.
  • Nothing recorded. Frames analyzed in memory and discarded.
  • Only numbers saved. Rep counts and XP in localStorage.
